Required Documents & Timeline Checklist

Required Document List (Category: Tourism)

  • Visa Application Form: Fully completed and signed.
  • Passport: Original passport valid for at least 3 months after departure, with at least 2 empty pages.
  • Passport Copy: Copy of the biometric details page and all stamped pages.
  • Photos: Two recent passport-size biometric color photos on a white background.
  • Travel Insurance: Valid for all Schengen countries covering medical/repatriation (min €30,000).
  • Proof of Accommodation: Confirmed hotel reservation or hospitality host declaration.
  • Proof of Financial Sufficiency: Bank statements for the last 3 months, proof of salary/income.
  • Flight Booking: Confirmed round-trip flight reservation.
